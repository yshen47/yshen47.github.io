Yuan Shen

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at UIUC, starting in Fall 2019. My PhD advisors are Prof. Katherine Driggs-Campbell and Prof. Shenlong Wang . My research area is in Computer Vision and Human Computer Interaction.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor's degree with the highest honor at UIUC, with a major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics. I was a research assistant at the Data-Driven Design group, led by Prof. Ranjitha Kumar. My undergrad research focused on fashion AI.
