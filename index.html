Research Interest
  • 3D Generation

I am a Computer Science PhD Candidate at UIUC. My PhD advisor is Prof. Shenlong Wang. I am widely interested in topics related to 3D generative modeling in computer vision. My past and ongoing projects involve scene-level generation, editing, 3D representation and 3D-related downstream application.

During my Phd, I am fortunate to collaborate with David Forsyth, Katie Driggs-Campbell, Wei-chiu Ma, Duygu Ceylan, Niloy Mitra, Anna Frühstück, Zexiang Xu, Paul Guerrero, Chuhang Zou, Xiaohan Fei, Bryan A. Plummer, Shengze Wang and many other amazing researchers.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor's degree with the highest honor at UIUC, with a major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics. I was a research assistant at the Data-Driven Design group, led by Prof. Ranjitha Kumar. My undergrad research focused on fashion AI.

I am co-organizing the external speaker seminar at UIUC vision group. If you are interested in presenting, please let me know!

Inspired by Wei-chiu Ma, I have decided to commit 1~2 hours every week to provide guidance, suggestions, and/or mentorships for students from underrepresented groups or whoever is in need. Please fill in this form if you are interested.
